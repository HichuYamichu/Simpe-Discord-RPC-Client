What do I do?

×
  1. go to this site and create your app (it's simple enough you should be fine)
  2. go to Rich Presence tab and add images you want your RPC to display
  3. copy your app ID from General Information tab
  4. fill your info as you wish (NONE of the fields are required except client id)
  5. your RPC should start working within 15 seconds after you've pasted client id
  6. you can save your info if you don't want to type it every time you start RPC
